STAND-UP COMEDY

Bringing wit and humour to the stage and ready to make audiences laugh and forget their troubles for a little while, we feel pleasure in presenting you Gurleen pannu. Gurleen, the runner-up of Comicstaan season 3, is a new-age stand-up comedian from the city of Chandigarh. With her ethnic roots and her keen sense of humour close to reality, her words strikes the right chords making you laugh. Her tragic comedy, everyday stories of a conventional Indian family and observational humour comes from her analytical gaze and her relatable personal anecdotes and wit are bound to leave you with a burst of breathless laughter.
